## Overview

The Adaptive Kalman + AMPC greenhouse project may have a public-facing website, landing page, or marketing content in a later version, but public content is not part of v1. v1 is focused on technical validation of the prediction plus Adaptive Kalman state-estimation pipeline and AMPC-ready modeling workflow.

**Primary value proposition**: The project turns noisy greenhouse measurements into traceable, evaluated Adaptive Kalman-ready state estimates that can support AMPC design for smarter irrigation, misting, and ventilation.
